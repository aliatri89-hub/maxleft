Privacy Policy

Effective: February 20, 2026

MANTL ("we," "our," or "us") operates the mymantl.app website and application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Account Information. When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo through Google OAuth. You may also provide a username, bio, location, and avatar.

Shelf Data. We store information about books, films, shows, and games you add to your shelves, including titles, ratings, notes, progress, and journal entries.

Activity & Social Data. We store friend connections, activity feed items, reactions, comments, and wishlist items you create through using the app.

Third-Party Integrations. If you connect Strava, we store your Strava access and refresh tokens to retrieve your activity data. We only access activity data you authorize.

Usage Data. We may collect basic usage information such as pages visited and features used to improve the service.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the MANTL service
  • To display your public profile at mymantl.app/[username]
  • To show your activity to friends who you have accepted
  • To generate your monthly recap cards
  • To retrieve fitness data from connected services like Strava
  • To improve and develop new features

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in these circumstances:

  • Public Profiles. Your username, bio, location, avatar, and shelf contents are visible at your public profile URL. You can control shelf visibility in settings.
  • Friends. Your activity feed is visible to users you have accepted as friends.
  • Service Providers. We use Supabase for database and authentication, Vercel for hosting, and third-party APIs (TMDB, Google Books, RAWG, Strava) to power app features.
  • Legal Requirements. We may disclose information if required by law.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security policies ensuring users can only access their own data. Authentication is handled through Supabase Auth with Google OAuth. All connections use HTTPS.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Edit or delete your profile information at any time
  • Remove any shelved items, journal entries, or comments
  • Disconnect third-party integrations like Strava
  • Control which shelves are visible on your public profile
  • Request deletion of your account by contacting us

Third-Party Services

MANTL integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google (OAuth authentication, Books API)
  • Supabase (database, authentication, storage)
  • TMDB (movie and TV show metadata)
  • RAWG (video game metadata)
  • Strava (fitness activity data, optional)
  • Vercel (hosting)

Children's Privacy

MANTL is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or by updating the effective date above.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to request account deletion, contact us at hello@mymantl.app.
