Delete Your Mantl Account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the app:

  1. Open Mantl and go to your Profile (bottom right)
  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account below Sign Out
  3. Confirm the deletion — this is permanent and cannot be undone

This will permanently delete your profile, shelves, activity history, Strava connection, and all associated data.

If you're unable to access the app or need assistance, contact us at hello@mantl.app and we'll process your request within 48 hours.
